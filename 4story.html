Our Story

In September 2014, Paweł and Paulina were both invited to a housewarming party. Neither of them were friends directly with the housemates, and both of them considered not going to the party but end up deciding to go.

Paulina was waiting in the foyer when Paweł walked in with his friends. They both noticed each other. When they ended up in the same apartment, they spent most of the night chatting, joking about Poland and Russia.

They went on their first date a week later.

In 2015, Paweł was accepted to the General Assembly coding bootcamp and moved in with Paulina in Alphabet City. In May 2015, Paulina graduated NYU and Paweł graduated GA.

Paweł and Paulina moved in together (officially) in Kensington, Brooklyn in September 2015. They lived there for a year and a half before moving to Williamsburg, Brooklyn, in February 2017.

Shortly after their 7th anniversary, Paweł and Paulina went on vacation to Acadia National Park, Maine. After a challenging hike up Cadillac Mountain for a sunset view, Paweł proposed to Paulina in the dense fog. They tried to hike down a different trail, but it got dark and they started getting lost, so they walked down the main road instead. Exhausted and excited, they returned to their AirBnB to share their news.

In September 2022, they will get married at Bear Mountain Inn, surrounded by their closest friends and family, followed by a honeymoon to Peru. They're very excited to start the next phase of their lives together!
