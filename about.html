About the Wedding

Bear Mountain Inn

3020 Seven Lakes Drive,
Tomkins Cove, NY 10986

Saturday, September 10, 2022

The ceremony starts at 6:00pm, with a cocktail hour and reception to follow.

Please plan to arrive by 5:45pm.

Please dress in cocktail attire.

This means tea-length, knee-length, or midi skirts or dresses; semi-formal jumpsuits; or suits and ties.

While the gift we want most is your presence, we also have a honeymoon fund available.

Since we've been living together for so long, and are planning to move soon, we'd prefer no physical gifts!

If you'd like to spend the night, we have two hotel blocks available:

You can stay directly at the Bear Mountain Inn - please call 845-786-2731 x.1000 to speak to reservations.

You can also stay at the Holiday Inn and Suites - West Point/Fort Montgomery by using that direct link, reserving online with the block code MFW , or by calling 877-864-0138 and mentioning the block name Miller-Fielek Wedding and code MFW .

We will arrange a shuttle from the Holiday Inn to the venue and back - please let us know if you would like a seat!

If you're staying the night, please join us at our morning-after brunch!

There are also many activities around Bear Mountain Inn - please visit our Around Bear Mountain tab for some ideas!

We're going to arrange some transportation - check back for more details soon!

You can drive to Bear Mountain Inn - parking is free after 5pm, or for overnight guests.

You can take the MetroNorth Hudson Line from Grand Central to the Peekskill train station.

There is also a bus available on the Shortline Hudson line from the Port Authority bus terminal with a stop at Bear Mountain Inn.

Unfortunately, we won't be able to have the dogs participate.

However, please visit our photos tab to see some pictures!

If your children are listed on your invitation, they're invited!

Please let us know when you RSVP if they will be attending.

Please feel free to text or call us with any questions you might have - we'd love to talk to you about our wedding!
